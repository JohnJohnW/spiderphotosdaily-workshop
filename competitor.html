About the Photographer

OsCorp Digital

Norman Osborn

Director, OsCorp Industries Pty Ltd — Photography Division

Norman Osborn is an entrepreneur, innovator and businessman based in Sydney, Australia, and New York City. As Director of OsCorp Industries, Norman brings a unique aerial perspective to city photography — capturing the energy of the urban landscape from heights most photographers never experience.

SpiderPhotosDaily.com was established in June 2022 to share Norman's original portfolio of Spider-Man inspired city and rooftop photography with audiences worldwide. All images featured on this site are part of OsCorp's original digital media catalogue.

For licensing, commercial use enquiries, or to discuss exhibition opportunities, please use the contact form below or reach the OsCorp Digital Media team directly.
