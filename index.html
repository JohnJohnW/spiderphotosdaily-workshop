About the Photographer

Peter Benjamin Parker

Sydney's Rooftop Photographer

Based in Sydney, NSW, Peter Parker is a professional freelance photographer with a passion for urban landscapes seen from impossible vantage points. Armed with permits, patience, and a head for heights, Peter has been documenting cityscapes from rooftops and aerial positions since early 2022.

His work spans two cities - Sydney and New York - capturing the geometry, light, and human rhythm of urban life from above. His images have appeared in architectural journals, travel publications, and corporate campaigns.

All photographs published on SpiderPhotos.com are original works created exclusively by Peter Parker and are protected under the Copyright Act 1968 (Cth).
